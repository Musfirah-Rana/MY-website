    Sketching

    Sketching is a fundamental artistic skill that involves creating quick,rough drawings to capture ideas,concepts or observations.It is often used as apreliminary step before developing a more detailed artwork,allowing artists toexperiment with compositions ,propositions,and perspective.Sketching can be done with varios tools such as pencils,charcoal,pens ,or digital devicesand it is widely used in feilds like illustration,architecture,fashion desighn,and engineering.Beyond its professional applications s, sketching is also a powerful form of personal expression and creativity,helping individuals visualise their thoughts and refine their artistic abilities.
